About Me

Born and raised in Providence, Rhode Island, I feel like I have came a long way. I went to Cornell University, but I had to withdraw after my junior year due to not having enough money to continue attending. However, I refused to let that stop me and decided to make my own path. I am very ambitious and I love to pave a way to success even when it seems impossible to do so. Have I also mentioned that I am super passionate about technology? Yeah, I am a big tech nerd, especially when it comes to subjects pertaining to Information Technology, which is better known as IT.

Ever since I got my first computer in middle school I was intrigued with how computers work. I would try out different ways that I could optimize my laptop to perform better, remain in pristine condition and keep it secure. However, it was not until the summer of 2018 that I decided to get my first IT certification: CompTIA Network+. Then, I proceeded to acquire my first AWS certification the following summer, which is the Solutions Architect certification below, and then my Linux+ certification during the summer of 2020. Now, I currently work on strengthening my skills in IT and programming through a variety of personal projects as well as continuing to acquire more IT certifications gradually. Feel free to check out some of my various projects by visiting the My Work page.

Here are some more fun facts about me: My favorite color is silver and I am also a freestyle urban dancer. I have been dancing since high school, and I have been self taught during my free time. When I do not have my face in front of a computer, or when I need a break from the stresses in life, I take the time to dance. I use dance as a creative outlet to free my mind and to keep myself moving during the day. Furthermore, I think Linux is better than Windows and I can speak French (Vous ne vous attendiez cela pas, hein?) Enough about me, you can learn more about me by connecting with me on the Contact page. From there you can send me an email and connect with me via LinkedIn or Instagram after sending me an email (that is where I have my dance videos).
