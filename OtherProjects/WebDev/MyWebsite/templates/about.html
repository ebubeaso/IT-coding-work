About Me


Born and raised in Providence, Rhode Island, I feel like I have came a long way. I went to Cornell University, but I had to withdraw after my junior year due to not having enough money to continue. However, I refused to let that stop me and decided to take my own path. I am very ambitious and I love to make a way when it seems impossible to make a way. Have I also mentioned that I am super passionate about technology? Yeah, I am a big tech nerd, especially for Information Technology.

Ever since I got my first computer in middle school I was intrigued with how computers work. I would try out different ways that I could optimize my laptop to perform better and remain at its pristine condition. However, it was not until the summer of 2018 that I decided to get my first IT certification: CompTIA Network+. From there, I proceeded to acquire my first AWS certification (The Solutions Architect one) and my Linux+. Now, I currently work on strengthening my skills in IT and programming through a variety of personal projects. Feel free to check some of them out in the My Work page.

Here are some more fun facts: My favorite color is silver and I am also a freestyle urban dancer. I have been dancing since high school, and I have been self taught ever since. When I do not have my face in front of a computer, I take the time to dance as a creative outlet and keep myself moving. In addition, I think Linux is better than Windows and I can speak French (Vous ne vous attendez cela, hein?) Enough about me, you can learn more about me by connecting with me on the Contact page, where you can send me an email or connect with me on my LinkedIn or my Instagram (that is where I have my dance videos).
