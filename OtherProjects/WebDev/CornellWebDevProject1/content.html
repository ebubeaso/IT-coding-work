EBUBE'S DANCE PERSPECTIVE: Growing As A Dancer

What To Expect

As you may have guessed, I will be posting dance videos on this site. However, here are some specifics that I wanted to get into:

  • I will be posting a new video every Monday and Thursday at 8pm.
  • Of the two videos I post each week, one will be a choreography to a song of my choice and the other will be a freestyle dancing.
  • With each new post I will give insight on what I have been working on as well as give some context about the choreography or the feelings that a song gave me while dancing to it.
  • Inspirational messages that can help those who need it.

Some Inspiration

  • September 9, 2018:Do not give up on a task or skill if it is challenging at first. Treat it like it is another person that you are starting to get to know and invest your time to invest a relationship with what skill you are trying to learn.

Through dance, the space around me is my canvas, my body is the paintbrush and the music is the paint. With these components I am capable of creating my unique form of visual art to showcase to the world! Whether I inspire one person or a multitude of people, it will be an honor to have my dance perspective mean a lot to another dancer working on their craft.
